Refund Policy

Our Refund Policy

At LuxeEssence, we prioritize customer satisfaction. We want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If you are not satisfied with your product for any reason, we are here to help. Please read our refund policy carefully to understand the terms and conditions.

  • Eligibility for Refund: You can request a refund within 30 days of receiving the product. The item must be unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition that you received it.
  • Non-refundable Items: Certain items such as opened skincare products or personalized items are not eligible for a refund for hygiene and safety reasons.
  • Proof of Purchase: To process your refund, we will require a receipt or proof of purchase.
  • Refund Processing Time: Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you. If approved, your refund will be processed within 7-10 business days.
  • Shipping Costs: Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund unless the product was defective or damaged.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund or return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@luxeessence.com with your order number and reason for return.
  2. Package your item securely, including all original packaging and any promotional items or gifts.
  3. Ship the package back to us at the address we provide in our return authorization email.
